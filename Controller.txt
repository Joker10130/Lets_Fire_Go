**controller**

1) start / stop / reset buttons
-start button for start spread of fire
-stop button for stop spread of fire
-reset button for reset 

2)probcatch show as percentage
-probCatch is the probability that the tree will catch fire at the next time step.

3)probtree show as percentage
- probTree is probability that a tree (burning or not burning) initially occupies a
  site. Thus, probTree is the initial tree density measured as a percentage.

4)probBurning show as percentage
- probBurning is the fraction of the trees that are burning when the simulation begins.
